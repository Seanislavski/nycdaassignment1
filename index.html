Why This is Such a Great Site

and how I'm super humble about it (cuz I'm great too)

by Sean Rivis


This is probably the greatest site. Let me mansplain. In this world there are a few things that are sure. They are:

  • Terrorism
  • Bad-Assness
  • Stuffed Mushrooms

Let's not forget the importance of what I say here. It's super important. I am important. Don't argue. I'm not usually wrong, I'm usually right. I'm not being defensive.

So let's get to dealing with it, cuz you're all upset and crying all the time. I just simply can't handle you crying so much. Please stop crying. Feelings!

I read an article the other day on Comic Sans and why it was so terrible. I truly didn't understand from a design point of view what was so stand-offish about the font. I found out through that site about this font, `Comic Neue`. I don't like it at all! Why is it so ugly? I can't tell. It is supposed to be more readable than Comic Sans though, so I suppose there's that!
